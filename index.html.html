OUTDOOR SCREEN

Outdoor led displays are large billboards made to display, advertise any kind of video, and more. They can be placed in any open-air area thanks to their modular assembling system. LED screens are cost-effective, reliable and offer a high return of your investment.

Benefits includes: High brightness LED screens, Water resistance, Weather-resistant LED display, Energy-efficient LED screens

Our screens come with our own special and intuitive software to manage them, which can be used to program multiple functions, like scheduling the on and off time, regulating contrasts and brightness, and more. In addition, you can upload all your content remotely, you will even be able to program the screen to display some ads or content during specific periods of time.

Indoor LED screens are lighter than other type of digital screens.
